Ha Long Bay, Vietnam

Ha Long Bay is a UNESCO World Heritage site, renowned for its emerald waters and thousands of limestone islands. It is one of Vietnam's most beautiful and picturesque destinations.

Top Attractions:

  • Ti Top Island - A popular spot for panoramic views of Ha Long Bay.
  • Sung Sot Cave - A massive cave with awe-inspiring formations.
  • Cat Ba Island - A nearby island for trekking, beaches, and wildlife.

Ha Noi City, Vietnam

Ha Noi is the capital of Vietnam, blending history with modernity. From its ancient temples to its French colonial architecture, there's always something to explore in this bustling city.

Top Attractions:

  • Hoan Kiem Lake & Ngoc Son Temple - A tranquil spot in the heart of Ha Noi.
  • Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum - The final resting place of Vietnam's revolutionary leader.
  • Temple of Literature - A historical site dedicated to Confucius and education.

Paris, France

Paris, the City of Light, is known for its iconic landmarks, world-class museums, and romantic ambiance. Whether you're visiting the Eiffel Tower or enjoying a café on the Champs-Élysées, Paris never disappoints.

Top Attractions:

  • Eiffel Tower - A global symbol of Paris and one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world.
  • The Louvre Museum - A must-see art museum, home to the Mona Lisa and thousands of masterpieces.
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral - A Gothic masterpiece that represents the heart of Paris.
